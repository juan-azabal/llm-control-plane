# Pricing — AcmeCo Analytics Platform

## Plans

### Free Plan — $0/month
- 1 dashboard
- 3 users
- 1-hour data refresh
- 12-month data retention
- Community support only

### Pro Plan — $49/month per user
- 10 dashboards
- 50 users maximum
- 15-minute data refresh
- 36-month data retention
- Salesforce integration
- Email support (24-hour response)

### Enterprise Plan — $99/month per user (annual billing required)
- Unlimited dashboards
- 500 users maximum
- 15-minute data refresh
- Unlimited data retention
- All integrations
- Priority support (4-hour response)
- Custom SSO (SAML 2.0)
- Dedicated account manager

## Billing
- Monthly billing for Free and Pro plans
- Annual billing required for Enterprise (10% discount applied)
- All prices in USD
- No setup fees
- 14-day free trial for Pro plan (no credit card required)

## Refund Policy
- Pro plan: full refund within 30 days of first payment
- Enterprise plan: prorated refund within 90 days
- No refunds after the refund window
